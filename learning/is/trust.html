Learning is trust.

Everything ever is hard, and you'll have to learn, over and over again, that you just need to trust the process.

It's just not possible for someone to tell you what they have learned. You have to learn it for yourself. In the meantime, you have to trust that if you do what they did, eventually, you'll understand, too.
